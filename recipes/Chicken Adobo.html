Chicken Adobo

Chicken Soy Sauce and Vinegar based stew

Chicken Adobo is FILIPINO dish that I ate a lot growing up, it is very delicious and best paired with warm rice

Ingridients:
  • Chicken
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Black Peppercorns
  • Bay Leaves
  • Brown Sugar
Steps on how to cook the perfect Chicken Adobo
  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a big pot. I used a Dutch oven. Add chicken thighs skin side down and sear on both sides.
  2. Once done searing the chicken, add the garlic and soy sauce. Mix to the coat the chicken.
  3. Add the vinegar, brown sugar, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Bring the liquid to a boil and lower to a simmer.
  4. Simmer on low heat for 1 hour. Flip the chicken every 20 minutes, so each side gets an even amount of color.
  5. Serve chicken over a bed of rice.
